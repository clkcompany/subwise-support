Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 11, 2026

Local-first data

The subscriptions, prices, billing cycles, renewal dates, categories, notes, family-plan details, and preferences you enter are stored on your device.

No account required

Subwise does not require sign in and does not upload your subscription list to a Subwise server. If cloud sync is added in the future, it will be presented as an optional feature.

Notifications

If you enable renewal reminders, Subwise asks your device for notification permission and schedules local reminders. Notification settings can be changed from your device settings.

Sharing and backups

When you use share, export, or import features, you choose what leaves your device. Shared backup text may include subscription names, prices, dates, notes, and family-plan details.

Purchases

If you buy Subwise Pro, purchase handling is provided by the app store. Subwise may receive purchase-status information needed to unlock Pro features.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact the developer through the support channel listed on the Subwise Support page.
